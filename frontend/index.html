About us

Welcome to Pierre Collet Motors

Welcome to Pierre Collet Motors where we pride ourselves on delivering friendly, honest and trustworthy customer service in conjunction with experienced mechanics and a range of quality used cars.

Established in 1975 Pierre Collet Motors has the knowledge as well as the latest diagnostic equipment and a fully equipped workshop to keep your cars running smoothly.

We specialise in and are passionate about selling and servicing Subarus whilst also offering a diverse selection of cars in our yard to purchase.
